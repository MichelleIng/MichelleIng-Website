With all the volunteering I’ve done, and being used to working early hour shifts, I’ve exemplified good time management skills as well. Being in the foster care system is never easy, especially for children that young of an age, and I’m happy I had the opportunity to be a part of this program to help them through that process.

Being both in a job and also volunteering I have learned a lot of professionalism from a lot of the adults who worked with me in these environments.
